# The Big Picture

This section provides a user friendly description and overview of the next version (v1.1) of Music Besties app. It is designed to be the guiding light for the rest of the documentation by having a complete description of how the app works at a high level.

# Text Overview

Music Besties is a web application designed to help music enthusiasts organize, rank, and review their favorite albums and songs. The application provides an intuitive interface for creating personalized music collections with custom rankings and detailed reviews, while also connecting users with other fans who share similar musical tastes.

In version 1.1, Music Besties offers these key features:

  • Dashboard View: Browse music eras with visual cards showing your rankings and ratings at a glance
  • Edit Rankings View: Select and organize your favorite songs with a simple tap interface and 24-hour cooldown between saves
  • Sidebar Panel: Access your journal, community content, and connect with "besties" who share your music taste
  • Bestie Matching: Find other fans with similar preferences through our matching algorithm
  • Community Features: Share your rankings and reviews, and discover what others think
  • Onboarding Experience: New users are guided through a streamlined setup process
  • History Tracking: See how your rankings have changed over time with versioned history
  • Social Integration: Verify through Reddit and share your content on social media

The app maintains the intuitive, clean interface from version 1.0 while adding social features that connect you with the wider music fan community. All interactions are designed to be simple and satisfying, with visual feedback that makes organizing your music preferences feel rewarding.

Whether you're a casual listener or a dedicated fan, Music Besties v1.1 provides tools to document your musical journey and connect with others who share your passion.
